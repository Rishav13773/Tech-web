ABOUT

Technology Solutions

Technology Solutions is the premier provider of outsourced IT services in the Southwest, including: Tucson, Phoenix, Albuquerque, and Santa Fe. Founded as separate companies in Arizona and New Mexico (Asset Technology, by Greg Nickolson in 2007, and Technology Solutions, by Carlos Gonzales in 2009, respectively) the companies expanded in their respective markets, before merging and becoming Technology Solutions in 2014.
